7-Day Meal Plan for Weight Loss and Muscle Gain

Looking to transform your body without spending hours counting calories or searching for the perfect meals? Our 7-day meal plan for weight loss and muscle gain is designed to help you achieve both goals simultaneously — lose fat and build lean muscle — using simple, delicious, and nutrient-dense meals.

Whether you're a beginner or just want to reset your diet, this weekly diet plan provides balanced macros, affordable ingredients, and flexible options tailored for real results. Plus, it's crafted with input from certified nutritionists and fitness coaches to support your journey toward better health.

In this guide, you'll find a complete day-by-day breakdown, a printable PDF version for easy tracking, and helpful tips to stay consistent without sacrificing taste. If you're ready to kickstart a smarter way of eating, this healthy meal plan is the perfect place to begin.

Why You Need a 7-Day Meal Plan

Following a 7-day meal plan isn’t just about having meals ready — it’s about building a consistent, healthy eating routine that supports your weight loss and muscle gain goals. When you plan your meals in advance, you eliminate last-minute decisions that often lead to unhealthy choices or skipped meals.

One of the main benefits of a weekly meal plan is that it ensures your body receives the right balance of protein, complex carbs, and healthy fats each day — the foundation of both fat loss and muscle building.

This structured approach also helps you stay within your calorie goals, avoid food waste, save money, and reduce stress around daily meal decisions. Whether you’re working out regularly or just starting your fitness journey, a meal plan for weight loss and muscle gain makes it easier to stay on track, stay full, and see real results.

7-Day Meal Plan for Weight Loss and Muscle Gain

This comprehensive meal plan covers all 7 days of the week, helping you stay on track without needing to overthink your meals. Each day is optimized for around 1800–2200 calories depending on your portion sizes, with a focus on high-protein, whole foods to support fat loss and lean muscle gain.

Day 1 (Monday)

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs, spinach, whole grain toast (350 kcal)
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with mixed berries (180 kcal)
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, quinoa, steamed broccoli (450 kcal)
  • Snack: Protein shake + banana (250 kcal)
  • Dinner: Baked salmon, sweet potato, green beans (500 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,730 kcal

Day 2 (Tuesday)

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats with chia seeds and almond milk (350 kcal)
  • Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter (200 kcal)
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with hummus and veggies (400 kcal)
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with cucumber (180 kcal)
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with brown rice and bell peppers (500 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,630 kcal

Day 3 (Wednesday)

  • Breakfast: Protein pancakes with honey and berries (370 kcal)
  • Snack: Boiled eggs + cherry tomatoes (170 kcal)
  • Lunch: Grilled shrimp, couscous, mixed greens (420 kcal)
  • Snack: Protein bar (200 kcal)
  • Dinner: Chicken stir fry with vegetables and brown rice (500 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,660 kcal

Day 4 (Thursday)

  • Breakfast: Avocado toast with poached eggs (360 kcal)
  • Snack: Mixed nuts (200 kcal)
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with olive oil and whole grain crackers (400 kcal)
  • Snack: Greek yogurt smoothie (220 kcal)
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with veggies and jasmine rice (520 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,700 kcal

Day 5 (Friday)

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with banana and almond butter (370 kcal)
  • Snack: Protein shake (180 kcal)
  • Lunch: Chicken Caesar wrap (450 kcal)
  • Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus (150 kcal)
  • Dinner: Grilled tilapia, mashed sweet potato, green beans (500 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,650 kcal

Day 6 (Saturday)

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with banana, berries, and granola (350 kcal)
  • Snack: Rice cakes with almond butter (190 kcal)
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with side salad (420 kcal)
  • Snack: Hard-boiled eggs + cucumber (170 kcal)
  • Dinner: Turkey meatballs with spaghetti squash (510 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,640 kcal

Day 7 (Sunday)

  • Breakfast: Chia pudding with mango and coconut flakes (340 kcal)
  • Snack: Edamame + green tea (180 kcal)
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken taco bowl with salsa (450 kcal)
  • Snack: Smoothie with protein and berries (220 kcal)
  • Dinner: Baked cod with roasted vegetables and wild rice (500 kcal)

Total Calories: 1,690 kcal

7-Day Meal Plan Summary (At a Glance)

  • Purpose: Fat loss + muscle gain using real, whole foods.
  • Duration: 7 days (Monday–Sunday).
  • Daily Calories: 1,600 – 1,800 kcal average per day.
  • Meals per day: 5 (3 main meals + 2 snacks).
  • Meal types: High-protein, moderate-carb, healthy fats.
  • Best for: Beginners, gym-goers, or anyone wanting a structured plan.

Final Thoughts & Tips for Success

Sticking to a structured meal plan like this one is one of the most effective ways to stay consistent and reach your health goals. But remember — it’s not about perfection. It’s about making smarter choices more often.

Feel free to swap ingredients, adjust portion sizes, and listen to your body. This plan is meant to guide you, not restrict you. Whether you’re trying to lose weight, gain muscle, or simply eat healthier, small steps make a big difference.
